================================================================================
PHASE A — PREMIUM TRUST + CONVERSION PSYCHOLOGY (IMPLEMENT FIRST)
================================================================================

Goal: When a couple lands on About, Contact, Weddings, Packages, or key pillars,
they should feel **calm certainty**, **premium fit**, and **low social risk**—without
spammy urgency or fake scarcity.

Design principles:
  • Reassurance before ask (what happens next, no-pressure framing already strong—extend).
  • **Specificity > adjectives** (Squamish-rooted, Sea-to-Sky, named planning lenses).
  • **Social proof placement** near decisions (reviews, AuthorityProofStrip patterns).
  • **Exclusivity as craft**, not elitism (“intentional calendar,” “right fit,” not
    “only 3 spots left” unless true).

Likely implementation targets (edit only where copy/UX audit finds gap):

  • `src/app/about/page.tsx` — “Why different” / trust architecture; optional short
    block linking to guides + reviews; tighten headline support lines for anxiety
    (timeline, communication, what if X goes wrong).

  • `src/app/contact/page.tsx` — Hero + “What happens next”; micro-copy on consult vs
    availability; reinforce same reassurance as reviews language.

  • `src/components/cta-duo.tsx` + `book-consult-tracked-link` /
    `check-availability-tracked-link` — Label consistency, surface naming for analytics.

  • `src/components/contact-book-consult-section.tsx`,
    `src/components/contact-page-cta-trio.tsx` — Ordering, subcopy, friction reduction.

  • High-traffic pillars: `src/app/weddings/page.tsx`, `src/app/packages/page.tsx`,
    `src/app/whistler-wedding-dj/page.tsx` — One pass for “why HSDJ” and anxiety-kill
    sentences **above the fold** and **above final CTA**.

  • `src/app/faq/page.tsx` — Bridge FAQs to human trust (“how Patrick works”) where
    it reduces fear, not more links for links’ sake.

Deliverables:
  A1. Copy audit checklist (per page): primary anxiety → on-page answer → CTA.
  A2. Implemented copy/layout tweaks preserving motion + premium surfaces.
  A3. Analytics: confirm key events still fire (`src/lib/analytics.ts`).

Success metrics (4–8 weeks): higher consult + availability CTR from About/Contact/
  Weddings; scroll depth on Contact; qualitative “feels premium” review.

================================================================================
PHASE B — VENUE-SPECIFIC AUTHORITY STORIES (EDITORIAL MOAT)
================================================================================

Goal: **Editorial atmosphere narratives** tied to venue *dynamics* (sound, pacing,
guest travel, weather/elevation psychology)—not duplicate venue SEO pages.

Content rules (non-negotiable):
  • No fabricated couple names, dates, or venues; label editorial vs documentary.
  • Tie to **Roomflow Method** / **Atmosphere Arc** where natural.
  • Internal link to relevant `/venues/[slug]`, guides, Whistler pillar.

Implementation pattern (reuse existing stack):
  • New routes under `src/app/stories/[slug]/page.tsx` (follow sea-to-sky story).
  • Register in `src/app/stories/page.tsx` story list + `src/app/sitemap.ts`.
  • JSON-LD: `storyArticleJsonLd` + breadcrumb (already in `json-ld.ts`).
  • Imagery: `src/config/site-images.ts` + `public/images/` (see Phase C).

Example themes (backlog, prioritize by search + brand):
  • Sea-to-Sky Gondola: arc of the night + elevation/guest energy.
  • Whistler: destination-weekend pacing vs single-day Squamish.
  • “When the floor never opens” — planning diagnosis piece (non-judgmental).

Deliverables:
  B1. Editorial calendar (1–2 drafts/month realistic).
  B2. Published stories with internal links into venue hub and pillars.
